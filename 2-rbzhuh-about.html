About us

WHO WE ARE?

 
 
As you develop your business, expand your family, think about succession or plan for retirement, your wealth needs will advance. Here at Rural Bank Of Zhuanghe Huitong Co., Ltd. Bank, our different services like wealth management, insurance, loan and savings account will help you to meet those needs over time. Having the correct expertise next to you to minimize and oversee risk, while understanding your wealth’s potential, requires a fluid methodology – a consistent service that connects you to highly trained, expert thinking. Our business is customer driven and we focus on working in association to deliver the correct answers. Since your needs change over time, you require a bank that is flexible and adaptable in its work. We always look forward to provide you with the best services, technologies, products and new thinking to improve your position.
 
 

Mission

Our mission is to give our corporate, retail, and investment customers with customized, high quality and competitively priced financial solutions. Through both our consistently expanding regularly enhanced range of cutting-edge banking solutions and our effective distribution channels, we are focused on delivering world-class products and services that reward our partners and clients, as well as our staff. We aim to earn the reliability of employees, clients and the community by working with integrity and decency consistently.
 
 
 
 
 
 

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading private financial foundation. We strive to achieve this goal through acquiring advanced, secured services, in line with the best financial practices. We also strive to be a financially reasonable, independent community bank that is striving to improve the quality of life of the group we serve and to satisfy the customer’s needs by offering a mass of products that are driven by a sales and service philosophy.
