PERSONAL SERVICES

 

Accident insurance

It’s a customizable policy that protects you from the outcome of injuries when you most need it: at work, in your free time or 24/7.

Travel insurance

It’s the ideal insurance policy for your travels which grants you assistance and flexibility in every unexpected circumstance.

Accidental emergency medical insurance

This policy covers those cases of a medical emergency, providing you with ensured medical treatment.

Home comprehensive insurance

An insurance policy that takes complete care of the space you live in, your home content and your personal belongings.

Domestic helper comprehensive insurance

The babysitter, the gardner and the domestic helper do a precious job for the family. It is therefore important to protect them against risks of injury. By underwriting this policy you will be able to secure your employees and to show your appreciation for the work they do for you and your family.

Personal Accident Insurance

Don’t let an injury or an illness affect your standard of living. With our personal accident insurance, Bank of China Group Insurance Company Limited protects you by developing a solution which best suits your lifestyle, in case an unexpected event would jeopardize your serenity.

Golfer Insurance

The risk of an accident while playing golf is always present. That is why every golfer needs insurance, no matter how careful or good they are at playing golf. Not only does this policy protect you in case of injury to third parties and damage to property, but it also covers your equipment against damage or theft.

Medical insurance

An insurance policy that directly speaks to you, who take your health to heart and want to safeguard it in a modular and customizable way. This policy offers you immediate support, quality services and the possibility to access to high standard medical facilities so that you can face an injury or an illness with more serenity.

BOC Family Medical insurance

BOC (Bank of China) family medical insurance policy gives you and your family protection for personal accident, annual travel, and home contents.

Outpatient insurance

This policy ensures that the expenses for consultations and treatments provided by a specialist or medical practitioner when hospitalization is not necessary.

Motor Insurance

Our motor insurance policy is flexible and customizable, designed to grant top safety, whichever your driving habits or demands are.

Hospital cash plan insurance

A policy that furnishes financial help during your hospital stay. You don’t have to worry about extra expenses, so you can face the period of recovery in a calmer way.
